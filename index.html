Mexico State, Mexico

Mexico State is a state in the Central Mexico. It surrounds Mexico City. It is bordered by Michoacan to the east, Queretaron and Hidalgo to the north, Tlaxcala and Puebla west, Mexico City, Morelos and Guerrero to the south. The state is a the main place in Mexico where there are archeological sites, one of the states, if not the state with most contrast economically, having some of the richest and poorest areas nationwide, one of the top states worldwide when it comes to crime and drugs, second only to Mexico City when it comes to infrastructure, transportation and industry. Important tourist destination for those who like archeology. I live in Tlalnepantla, one of the northern cities, with my Stake being one of the oldes in my country.
